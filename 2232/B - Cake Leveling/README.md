However, she is in a rush, so the frosting on the cake is uneven. To quickly solve this issue, Alice will put her knife at some <span class="tex-font-style-bf">integer</span> height and then sweep the frosting from left to right to make the frosting level.</p><p>Formally, let $$$a_i$$$ be the height of the frosting at the $$$i$$$-th position. Suppose that Alice puts her knife at some <span class="tex-font-style-bf">integer</span> height $$$h$$$. Excess frosting on position $$$n$$$ will be pushed off the cake completely. </p><p>Alice and her friends really love cake frosting. Since Alice might decide to serve some prefix of the cake instead of the whole cake, help her find the maximum height the frosting can be while keeping the frosting level for the first $$$i$$$ positions for $$$i = 1, 2, \ldots, n$$$.</p></div><div class="input-specification"><div class="section-title">Input</div><p>Each test contains multiple test cases.

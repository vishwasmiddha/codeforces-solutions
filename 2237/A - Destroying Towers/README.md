The height of the $$$i$$$-th tower is $$$a_i$$$. He wants vengeance for the destruction of his ecosystem, and has vowed to wreak as much havoc as possible with his laser gun.</p><p>Quack will operate on each tower <span class="tex-font-style-bf">exactly once</span>, in any order he chooses. The operation on tower $$$i$$$ is as follows:</p><ul> <li> Quack climbs to the top of tower $$$i$$$ and shoots a laser to the right, cutting the first taller tower it hits down to the same height as tower $$$i$$$.<p>Formally, let $$$j$$$ be the smallest index such that $$$j \gt i$$$ and $$$a_j \gt a_i$$$, where $$$a_i$$$ and $$$a_j$$$ are the current heights of the towers. Otherwise, nothing happens. </p></li></ul><p>Find the minimum possible final sum of tower heights over all possible orders of operations.</p></div><div class="input-specification"><div class="section-title">Input</div><p>Each test contains multiple test cases.
